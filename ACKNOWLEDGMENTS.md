# Acknowledgments

## Apple MLX

MLX-Zig is a ground-up rewrite of [Apple's MLX](https://github.com/ml-explore/mlx)
framework in Zig. The original MLX was developed by Awni Hannun, Jagrit Digani,
Angelos Katharopoulos, and Ronan Collobert at Apple. We are grateful for their
work in creating an open-source array framework for Apple Silicon.
